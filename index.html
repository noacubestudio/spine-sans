spine sans combinator

Spine Sans is a typeface split into halves, featuring a uniquely regular horizontal rhythm to function as shelves for generative effects.

First, pick a combination of the two styles. Then, explore a selection of computer-generated vector effects applied on whole words or per letter. Try typing text on the left!
